apophenia engine

 

what: a research project that bashed together 2 primitive 3D objects, and forced them to create a child

how: the digital tool used was photogrammetry in Autodesk Recap 360. The software was 'misused' into forcing the fusion of 2 objects instead of creating a mesh model of a single physical object

when: this project was completed in 2018 at the University of Calgary
